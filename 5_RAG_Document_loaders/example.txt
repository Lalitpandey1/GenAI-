In a world of wires and codes,
Where machines think and minds implode,
Artificial intelligence reigns supreme,
A futuristic dream, or so it seems.

With algorithms and data galore,
AI opens up new doors,
Predicting trends and solving problems,
It's a whirlwind of information, a system that hums.

But with progress comes fear,
Of a future unclear,
Will robots replace human touch,
Or simply compliment us, a crutch.

As we navigate this brave new age,
Let us remember the power we wage,
To control our creations, to ensure,
That artificial intelligence is a force for good, pure.

So let us embrace this technology,
But not forget our humanity,
For in the end, it's our hearts and souls,
That make us whole, that make us bold.
